I want to brainstorm an app idea with you. I'll lay it out, and please help me develop it further by asking me questions. Let's focus on the idea first, and then we can go into the more technical stuff.

Throughout our lives, we experience several moments of happiness, or when something very funny or memorable happened. These may be very short lived moments. You smile, laugh etc., but it may be so fast or short you may not even manage to take a photo or video of the moment. So what ends up happening is the moment gets forgotten. Someone may bring it up at some point in the future like "remember when...", but it's not stored somewhere. And with time, details of it may fade away, or it may be completely forgotten. I think it would beautiful if people had a tool to capture these moments. It's not something people will be able to do in the moment - the moment should be enjoyed to the fullest. But perhaps shortly after the moment, while the memory is fresh in their minds, or even weeks, months, years later when someone brings it up, they would still be able to note down some of the details. Perhaps, even be able to find any photos or videos around or during the moment, and upload them together with their notes. A moment should be shared among all people involved (owners / editors), or even others who weren't present, but would still like to know about it (readers).

For now, I feel like the app should be called "moments", but I'm open to ideas. I'm not sure what this looks like, but I have. Few things in mind:

* It should work well on both laptops and smartphones

* Users sign up with their first name, last name, email, and select a username. Checks are in place to prevent the same username being used multiple times. There should also be an option to upload a profile pic

* A user's home page should have an overview of all of their moments. Perhaps they should have the option to sort these in different ways, either chronologically (ascending or descending), or split as "Created by you", and "Shared with you". They should also be able to filter by date, location, people involved, or specific keywords / tags

* A user should be able to create a moment through a modal. This would capture standard info like moment name, date, location, people involved. Only Moment name should be mandatory field. Also the date field should somehow allow users to just capture the year of the moment, or only month and year, or a standard date (day, month, year) - depending on how much they remember about the moment

* Moment cards on the home page should be editable through a small edit icon. Every user mentioned in the moment should be able to make edits (more on this later). Every time someone edits a moment card, or the contents of a moment, all mentioned users should be notified

* A user can share a moment only with people they are friends with. If someone shares / mentions / tags a user in their moment, then all of those users become owners / editors of that moment. However, the users tagged / mentioned in a moment would receive a notification first alerting them that a friend mentioned them on a moment, and they have to accept or decline them being included. If all mentioned users decline, they moment can still exist in the originator user's account

* A user's home page there should be an Add friend functionality that allows them to search by a user's first name, last name, or username to find users, and add them as friends. This should send a friend request to the appropriate user (which they have to accept or reject). Since people can have friends, there also needs to be a Manage friends section somewhere to allow viewing friends, adding more friends, or deleting friends

* An Account page should also be available for each user. This is where they can edit their personal details, upload / edit their profile photo, and delete their account. Deleting an account would delete all of a user's data, but any moments they shared with other users should remain in those users' accounts. There should also be a notifications section allowing users to select or deselect if they want to receive notifications for new friend requests, moment invites / mentions, moment edits (posts etc.)

* Once a moment is created, the originator and mentioned users (once they accept) are able to type in a text box the details of the moment. Users can post multiple times, and each should appear as an individual post with details on who posted in, and when. There should be an option to sort posts in ascending or descending order based on the date posted, or filter posts by user. Posts should be able to also accommodate photos, videos, and voice recordings related to the moment - perhaps the modal allowing a user to add text details, also provides the ability to upload media

* There should also be a functionality within a moment page to invite readers. These are users who can view the moment contents, but they cannot make any edits. These should be already be friends with all users mentioned in the moment, and be searchable through their first name, last name, or username

This should form a strong foundation / basis for allowing users to capture their moment. I have additional ideas that we could layer on top of this (e.g., at the top of each moment page, a short AI summary of the moment based on all moment posts), but let's focus on the core idea for now
