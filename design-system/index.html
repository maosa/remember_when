Design System

Remember When

A private, collaborative memory book. Every design decision serves one goal: the people who lived a moment coming together to make sure it's never forgotten. Calm. Intimate. Warm.

Colours

Warm linen and stone neutrals form the canvas. Sage-teal is the single personality colour. Dusty blue carries informational states sparingly.

Typography

Lora brings warmth and elegance to emotional content. DM Sans keeps the UI clean and readable. The contrast between them is intentional — it marks the boundary between the product and the memory.
